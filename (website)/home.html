Everything You Need to Train Smarter

From AI-powered coaching to detailed analytics, Musclog gives you the tools to take full control of your fitness journey.

AI Coach (Loggy)

Chat with your AI fitness coach for personalized workout plans, nutrition advice, and meal photo estimation.

Barcode Scanner

Quickly add products by scanning barcodes. Access the Open Food Facts database with millions of products.

Weekly Check-ins

Automated 7-day analysis of your progress with dynamic goal adjustments to keep you on track.

Progress Analytics

Interactive charts for weight, body fat, and lifting progress. Track your TDEE with real data.

Cycle Awareness

Track menstrual phases and receive training recommendations optimized for your cycle.

Workout Templates

Create custom routines like PPL or Upper/Lower. Access an extensive exercise library with muscle targeting.

Health Connect

Sync with Google Health Connect to share data with other health and fitness apps on Android.

Encrypted Storage

Your sensitive health data is encrypted at rest using AES. Export anytime as JSON or Excel.
