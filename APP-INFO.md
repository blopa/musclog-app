## Description

> Max 4000 characters. Used on both iOS App Store and Google Play.

Musclog is your complete fitness companion — built for lifters, athletes, and anyone serious about their health. Track every workout, log your nutrition, monitor your progress, and get AI-powered coaching, all without sacrificing your privacy.

**WORKOUT TRACKING**

- Log strength, cardio, flexibility, and calisthenics sessions
- Build and reuse custom workout templates
- Track supersets and exercise groups
- Reorder exercises during or after a session
- Rest timer with haptic feedback
- Import old workouts by pasting text — AI parses and creates sessions for you

**NUTRITION & FOOD LOGGING**

- Search Open Food Facts, USDA, and local databases
- Scan barcodes or snap a photo — AI and on-device OCR identify the food for you
- Track calories, protein, carbs, fat, and fiber
- Get AI-generated meal plans tailored to your goals
- Daily nutrition summary with at-a-glance progress

**AI COACH**

- Chat with an AI coach powered by OpenAI and Google Gemini
- Get personalized advice on training and nutrition
- AI-powered progress insights and recommendations
- Customize coaching style with your own system prompts

**PROGRESS & ANALYTICS**

- Track body weight, body fat %, FFMI, and body measurements
- Monitor your real TDEE derived from your own logged data
- Visualize nutrition trends, workout volume, and body composition over time
- Correlate mood, training load, recovery, and macros
- Weekly check-ins with trend analysis and goal tracking

**HEALTH INTEGRATIONS**

- Sync with Apple Health (HealthKit) on iOS
- Sync with Google Health Connect on Android
- Read and write workouts, nutrition, weight, body fat, lean mass, and more

**MENSTRUAL CYCLE TRACKING**

- Log and track your cycle
- Correlate cycle phases with training performance and energy levels

**HOME SCREEN WIDGETS (Android)**

- Nutrition Progress widget: daily calorie and macro status at a glance
- Smart Camera widget: open the AI food scanner instantly

**PRIVACY FIRST**

- All data stored locally on your device — no account required
- Sensitive metrics encrypted at rest

**MORE**

- Metric and imperial unit support
- Mood tracking correlated with workouts and nutrition
- Focus mode for distraction-free sessions
- Export your data to spreadsheets
- Available in multiple languages

Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete, Musclog gives you the tools to train smarter and stay consistent. Lift, log, repeat.
