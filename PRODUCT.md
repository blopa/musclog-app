# Musclog Product Overview

Musclog is an all-in-one fitness companion designed to streamline workout logging, nutrition tracking, and health monitoring. It leverages AI to provide personalized coaching and insights, all while maintaining user privacy through local-first data storage.

## Core Features

### 1. Dashboard & Daily Summary

- **At-a-glance Overview**: See your daily caloric intake vs. goal, macro breakdown, and recent activities directly on the home screen.
- **Quick Actions**: Start a workout or log food with a single tap.
- **Personalized Greeting**: Dynamic greetings based on the time of day.

### 2. Workout Tracking & Scheduling

- **Comprehensive Logging**: Record sets, reps, weight, and rest times for every exercise.
- **Templates**: Create custom workout routines (e.g., PPL, Upper/Lower) for quick reuse.
- **Exercise Library**: Access a vast library of exercises with descriptions, muscle group categorizations, and equipment types.
- **Scheduling**: Plan your training week and receive reminders to stay consistent.
- **Volume Tracking**: Visualize your lifting volume and progress over time to ensure progressive overload.

### 3. Nutrition & Food Tracking

- **Extensive Database**: Search for foods using the Open Food Facts database or add your own.
- **Barcode Scanner**: Quickly add products by scanning their barcodes.
- **Macro & Micronutrients**: Track calories, protein, carbs, and fats, plus over 40 vitamins and minerals.
- **Meal Templates**: Save frequent meals (e.g., "Standard Breakfast") to log them in seconds.
- **Retrospective Logging**: Easily add logs for past days to keep your history complete.

### 4. Weekly Progress Check-ins

- **Automated Analysis**: Every week, Musclog analyzes your 7-day rolling average for weight, caloric intake, and activity levels.
- **Status Updates**: Get clear feedback on your progress:
  - **On Track**: You're meeting your targets.
  - **Ahead**: You're progressing faster than planned.
  - **Behind**: You might need to adjust your approach.
- **Dynamic Goal Adjustment**: Based on your check-in results, Musclog can help you recalculate your nutritional goals to keep you moving toward your target weight.

### 5. Menstrual Cycle Tracking & Awareness

- **Phase Monitoring**: Track menstrual, follicular, ovulation, and luteal phases.
- **Training Optimization**: Receive recommendations to adjust workout intensity based on your current cycle phase (e.g., deloading during the menstrual phase).
- **Contraceptive Support**: Configurable for both hormonal and non-hormonal birth control.

### 6. AI Coach (Loggy)

- **Dual Provider Support**: Choose between Google Gemini or OpenAI models.
- **Conversational Interface**: Chat with Loggy for advice on workouts, nutrition, or general fitness questions.
- **AI Vision**:
  - **Meal Estimation**: Take a photo of your food, and Loggy will estimate the portion size and nutritional content.
  - **Label Scanning**: Scan nutrition labels to automatically extract macro information.
- **Workout Generation**: Ask the AI to create a custom workout plan based on your available equipment and goals.
- **Contextual Memory**: The AI remembers your preferences and past interactions to provide more relevant advice over time.

### 7. Progress Analytics & Body Metrics

- **Visual Trends**: Interactive charts for weight, body fat %, and lifting progress.
- **Custom Metrics**: Track any measurement important to you (e.g., waist circumference, arm size).
- **TDEE Estimation**: Empirical calculation of your Total Daily Energy Expenditure based on your actual activity and weight changes.

### 8. Integrations & Data Management

- **Google Health Connect**: Sync weight, nutrition, and exercise data with other health apps on Android.
- **Data Portability**: Export your entire history as an encrypted JSON file or a human-readable Excel sheet.
- **Widgets**: Home screen widgets for quick logging and daily summary views.

### 9. Privacy & Security

- **Local-First**: Your data stays on your device. No cloud syncing of personal health data unless explicitly configured (e.g., AI features).
- **Encryption**: Sensitive metrics and nutrition logs are encrypted at rest using AES.
- **Offline-Ready**: Most core features work perfectly without an internet connection.
